MISSION STATEMENT

The St. John's Institute Commission on Elections commits to serve as an outlet to promote a sense of unity within the school. We shall develop and administer justified regulations relative to the conduct of the election to ensure effectiveness, honesty and order as we work to empower the Johnian community to be part of growth and strength.

VISION STATEMENT

The St. John's Institute Commission on Elections envisions a fair, and pragmatic system that is mounted upon the Johnian core values, committed to conducting transparent processes that support the truth in providing credible results while upholding integrity and honesty.
